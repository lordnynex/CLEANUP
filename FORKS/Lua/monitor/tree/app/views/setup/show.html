Anonymously report usage

You can run your APItools monitor completely standalone if you don't choose to pair it with the cloud service. However, if you're willing the you can help us out by reporting anonymized traffic stats. This will allow us to do performance analysis of various APIs, predict performance issues or just tell you if it is broken just for you or everyone else.
